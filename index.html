About Our Unit

The Rover & Ranger Unit of N.S.C.B.M. Govt. College, Hamirpur (established 2007), aims to empower youth through discipline, leadership, community service, and the values of the Bharat Scouts and Guides. We nurture character, citizenship, and commitment toward the betterment of society.
