About Our Unit

The Rover & Ranger Unit of N.S.C.B.M. Govt. College, Hamirpur (est. 2007) aims to empower youth through leadership, discipline, and community service. Operating under Bharat Scouts and Guides, Himachal Pradesh, our Unit inspires young minds to live by the motto “Service.”

Read More →

History of Scouting & Guiding

Scouting began in 1907 under Lord Robert Baden-Powell and reached India in 1909. In 1950, all associations unified as the Bharat Scouts & Guides.

Our Achievements

The Rover & Ranger Unit has earned recognition at district, state, and national levels.

  • 🌿 Environmental Awareness & Swachhta Campaigns
  • 🤝 Participation in Messenger of Peace and World Scarf Day
  • 🎓 Consistent representation in BSG HP Rover-Ranger Meets
