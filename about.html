Our Mission & Values

The Rover & Ranger Unit is dedicated to fostering the holistic development of young adults, transforming them into self-reliant, responsible, and compassionate citizens. Our mission is rooted in the core principles of the Scout and Guide movement: Duty to God, Duty to Others, and Duty to Self. We strive to instill leadership, discipline, integrity, and a lifelong spirit of selfless service to the community and the nation.
